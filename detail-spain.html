The Retiro is Madrid's most important park and, since opening to the public in 1868, it has become a haven of history and peace. Find out what to see in Retiro Park, the heart of Madrid. The Retiro is the most important park in Madrid and one of the favorite places for both locals and tourists. It is a large green space of 118 hectares located in the heart of Madrid.



    Sights in Retiro Lake:
  • The artificial lake is one of the first images that we will see if we enter through Puerta de Alcalá.
  • Monument to Alfonso XII: An impressive monument located on one of the shores of the former lake, it was inaugurated in 1922.
  • Palácio de Cristal: Built in 1887 in front of an artificial lake, the Palácio de Cristal is home to many temporary exhibitions.
  • Tour of Argentina: Popularly called the Tour of the Statues, it is a tour where we will find statues of different kings of Spain.
