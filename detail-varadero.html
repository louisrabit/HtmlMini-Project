Varadero, which straddles Cuba's narrow Hicacos Peninsula, is a popular beach resort.
Along its 20 km of coastline on the Atlantic Ocean, there is a range of all-inclusive hotel complexes and spas, as well as a golf course.
Near the southern end of the peninsula is the Varahicacos Ecological Reserve, a preserved area with trails and an ancient burial cave. Parque Josone has a lake and gardens .
